About Us

Rockford Dev is a local group for software developers and enthusiasts in Rockford, Illinois. Founded in 2024, this group strives to promote professional development and technical learning. We meet on the third Wednesday of every month at 6PM, on the second floor of the Rockford Public Library Main branch, in the WREX classroom.

Rockford Dev meetups often include an educational lecture or topic of discussion. Some of our past lecture topics have included:

  • GraphVis
  • Duplicate customer accounts
  • Google AppScript
  • The Fediverse
  • Creating this website

If you would like to host a lecture, please email rockforddev@googlegroups.com with a suggested outline.
